About De Guide Global Recycling Venture limited

Transforming Waste into Value. Building a Greener Future.

Who We Are

De Guide Global Recycling Venture Limited (DGGRV) is a forward-thinking recycling company dedicated to environmental sustainability and responsible waste management. We specialize in collecting and recycling aluminum cans, plastic bottles, cartons, and scrap metals from homes, businesses, and demolition sites.

Our mission is to reduce environmental pollution while creating economic value from recyclable materials. Through structured collection systems and strategic partnerships, we help individuals and organizations dispose of recyclable materials responsibly.

Our Mission

To create a cleaner environment by efficiently collecting, processing, and recycling reusable materials while empowering communities through sustainable waste solutions.

Our Vision

To become a leading recycling enterprise recognized for innovation, integrity, and environmental impact across Nigeria and beyond.
