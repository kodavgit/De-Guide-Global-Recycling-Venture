About Our Recycling Company

We are a professional recycling company committed to transforming waste into valuable resources. We collect aluminum cans, plastic bottles, cartons, scrap iron, and other recyclable materials from individuals, dump sites, companies, and demolition buildings.

Instead of throwing away items that still have value, we give individuals and businesses the opportunity to earn money from recyclable waste. We also provide bulk pickup services and demolition scrap collection across different locations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about selling to us.

Showing 7 Questions

What materials do you buy?

We buy aluminum cans, plastic bottles, cartons, scrap iron and other metal waste.

Do you provide pickup services?

Yes. We provide pickup services for large quantities within our coverage area.

How do I receive payment?

Payment is made immediately after weighing your materials via cash or bank transfer.

Is there a minimum quantity required to sell?

No strict minimum for drop-off. However, pickup services may require a reasonable quantity depending on your location.

Which areas do you operate in?

We operate across Lagos and surrounding regions. Contact us to confirm availability in your area.

Do you collect scrap from demolition sites?

Yes. We work with construction companies and property developers to recover scrap iron and recyclable materials from demolition sites.

Is your recycling process environmentally safe?

Absolutely. We follow environmentally responsible recycling practices to reduce pollution and support sustainability.
