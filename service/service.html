Driving Environmental Sustainability Across Nigeria

Professional Recycling Solutions

At De Guide Global Recycling Venture Limited (DGGRV), we deliver structured, efficient, and environmentally responsible recycling solutions for individuals, businesses, construction sites, and institutions. Our services are designed to reduce landfill waste, recover valuable materials, and promote a circular economy system that benefits both the environment and the economy.

Our Core Services

Aluminum Can Recycling

We collect, compress, and process used beverage cans into reusable aluminum materials for manufacturing industries. Aluminum recycling reduces energy consumption by up to 95% compared to new production.

Plastic Bottle Recovery

Our plastic recycling system ensures proper sorting, shredding, and processing of PET bottles into reusable raw materials for packaging and textile production.

Scrap Metal & Iron Recycling

We recover scrap irons from demolition sites and industrial facilities, converting them into valuable reusable metal resources for manufacturing.

Carton & Packaging Recycling

Recycling paper cartons and packaging materials into reusable pulp helps reduce deforestation and supports sustainable packaging solutions.

Bulk Waste Collection

We provide scheduled and on-demand waste pickup services for offices, estates, commercial buildings, and construction sites.

Waste Buy-Back Program

Individuals and businesses can sell recyclable materials directly to DGGRV, creating income opportunities while supporting environmental protection.

Corporate & Industrial Recycling Programs

DGGRV partners with corporations, educational institutions, real estate developers, and manufacturing companies to implement structured waste management programs.

  • On-site waste audits
  • Dedicated recycling bins
  • Environmental impact reporting
  • Scheduled bulk pickups
